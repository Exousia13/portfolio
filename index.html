About Me

Hi, I’m Matthew Olapade—a researcher, data analyst, and virtual assistant with a strong foundation in Statistics and Data Science. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from the University of Ibadan, along with advanced certifications in Financial Modeling & Valuation Analysis (FMVA) and Business Intelligence & Data Analytics (BIDA) from the Corporate Finance Institute (CFI).

I specialize in conducting research and extracting meaningful insights from complex data, helping businesses and individuals make informed decisions. With 8+ years of experience in transport, logistics, and retail, I have assisted companies in leveraging data-driven strategies through:

  • In-depth research (Research Proposals, Journals, Dissertations, and Theses)
  • Statistical analysis & data visualization
  • Identifying hidden trends & optimizing workflows

Beyond data analytics, I offer virtual assistance for research projects, supporting businesses, academics, and professionals with tasks such as data collection, market research, and comprehensive reporting. My ability to translate complex information into clear, strategic insights makes me a valuable asset to any team.

Looking for a dedicated researcher, data analyst, or virtual assistant to support your next project? Let’s connect—reach out via email, and let’s discuss how I can assist you!
